About Us


At Dream Breeze Travel, we bring a fresh and dedicated approach to crafting your travel dreams. Although we may be newcomers in the industry, our passion and commitment rival that of seasoned veterans. We believe that every journey is an opportunity to create memories that last a lifetime, and we are devoted to ensuring that your travel experience is nothing short of extraordinary.

Our Commitment to You


We take immense pride in our customer-centric philosophy. Our mission is to serve you with the highest level of attention, care, and professionalism. Although we're a family-owned business, we approach each client as an extension of our own family. Your desires and preferences are at the heart of every itinerary we create.

Transparency and Trust


Transparency is the cornerstone of our relationship with you. We believe in clear communication and complete honesty. You can trust that every detail of your journey will be shared openly, empowering you to make informed decisions and enabling us to build a strong foundation of trust together.

Comprehensive Travel Offerings


As your travel companion, we offer a diverse range of services to cater to your every need. From the elegance of Celebrity Cruises and Royal Caribbean to the innovation of Virgin Voyages and the fun of Carnival, our cruise line partnerships provide options that suit every taste. We also collaborate with American Airlines and more to ensure your flights are seamless and comfortable.

Magical Experiences Await


For the families seeking enchantment, we're an authorized Disney Travel and Universal Parks & Resorts. These magical worlds are our playgrounds too, and we understand how to create unforgettable experiences for families of all sizes.

All-Inclusive Convenience


Our offerings extend beyond cruises and flights. We provide access to vacation resorts that cater to various interests, tickets to captivating events that add a touch of excitement to your journeys, and car rentals to give you the freedom to explore. With us, you have access to a world of convenience, all in one place.

Travel with confidence knowing that we value your safety. We offer travel insurance options that provide protection and peace of mind, ensuring that unexpected situations don't overshadow your adventure.
