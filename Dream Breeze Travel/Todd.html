Unlocking the World with Todd and Dream Breeze Travel


Welcome to a world where wanderlust is our guiding star and personalized service is our compass. I'm Todd Taylor, and along with the passionate team at Dream Breeze Travel, we're here to turn your travel dreams into extraordinary realities. With a fervent love for exploration and a deep-seated commitment to your satisfaction, we're not just travel agents we're your partners in crafting unforgettable journeys. Our journey began with a simple yet profound belief: that every traveler deserves more than just a vacation; they deserve an immersive experience that leaves a mark on their soul. Our family-owned business roots run deep, and we understand the importance of making memories with loved ones. That's why we specialize in curating enchanting family getaways, each designed to foster bonding, laughter, and cherished moments. As ardent advocates for transparency, we believe in sharing every detail of your travel experience with honesty and integrity.

Your trust is our currency, and we're dedicated to ensuring that you embark on your journey with clarity and confidence. From the elegance of Celebrity Cruises and Royal Caribbean to the innovation of Virgin Voyages and the pure fun of Carnival, our partnerships with premier cruise lines promise voyages that transcend the ordinary. And when it comes to taking to the skies, our collaborations with American Airlines and more ensure that your flight to your dream destination is just as magical. We've also been granted the privilege of being an authorized Disney Travel and Universal Parks & Resorts agent, allowing us to sprinkle a touch of magic onto your family vacations. Beyond destinations and itineraries, we're about making your travel experience seamless and complete.

From flights and accommodations to car rentals and even tickets to captivating events, we've assembled a collection of services that cater to every facet of your journey. Join us as we embark on a journey together, where your aspirations meet our expertise, and the result is an adventure that exceeds your expectations. With Dream Breeze Travel, the world is yours to explore, and we're here to make it an unforgettable voyage.
